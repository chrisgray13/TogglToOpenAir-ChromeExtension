Welcome to the Toggl to OpenAir timesheet extension. Please set the information below and click sync time records!

API key is found within your Toggl profile.

The start date is the date timesheet entries are pulled from Toggl. An end date is automatically set as six days from the start date to pull a full week.

Use the round time checkbox to round your time entries to the nearest quarter hour, i.e. 15 minutes, with any entry below 7.5 minutes rounding up to 15 minutes.

Use the group by task checkbox to group your time entries by project, task, and time type; otherwise, by project, task, time type, and description.

Just getting started? Open a completed timesheet and simply click Import Projects to import projects into Toggl. Need more help? Click Help!
